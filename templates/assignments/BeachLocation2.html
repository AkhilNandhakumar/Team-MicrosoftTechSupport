Del Mar Beach

Notable People of Del Mar Beach

Theodore M. Loop

Loop was born in Michigan in 1938. He was a railroad official who was married to a woman named Ella. Ella later named the beach Del Mar.

Bayard Taylor

Bayard Taylor was born January 11, 1825 and died December 19, 1878. He was an American poet, literary critic, translator, travel author, and diplomat. Del Mar was given its name sake after being inspired by his poem "The Fight of Paso del Mar."

Enoch Talbert

Enoch Carl Talbert was born on 16 July 1896, in Stanly, North Carolina, United States. He is most commonly known for buying the land of Del Mar.
