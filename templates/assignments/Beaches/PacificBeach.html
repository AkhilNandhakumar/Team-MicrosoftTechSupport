Pacific Beach

Located just south of Crystal Pier, Pacific Beach extends south before becoming Mission Beach. One of the busiest beach areas in San Diego, Pacific Beach draws large crowds of locals and tourists in the summer. The two most popular activities at Pacific Beach beaches are swimming and surfing. Ocean Front Walk is a 2.3-mile paved walkway simply referred to as “the boardwalk” by locals. It runs from the north end of North Pacific Beach to the jetty at the south end of Mission Beach. Near Crystal Pier begins the row of numerous bars, restaurants, and shops, including places for bike rentals. (source: City of San Diego, and La Jolla)
